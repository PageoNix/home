Our Story

PageoNix was founded in 2024 by Avijit Singh (@DevLord-Avijit), a passionate full-stack developer and digital entrepreneur with over 8 years of experience building high-performance web applications.

After working with dozens of startups and enterprises, Avijit noticed a critical gap in the market: most web development agencies prioritized speed of delivery over quality, resulting in slow, insecure, and poorly designed websites that didn't drive real business results.

PageoNix was created to change this. We combine enterprise-grade engineering excellence with premium design and AI-powered automation to deliver digital products that are not just beautiful and fast, but also secure and scalable.
