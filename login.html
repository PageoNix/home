Frequently Asked Questions About PageoNix Login

How do I create a PageoNix account?

Visit our sign-up page, enter your email and create a password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. Once verified, you can immediately start building with PageoNix.

What if I forgot my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password within minutes.

Is my account data secure?

Absolutely. We use military-grade AES-256 encryption, SSL/TLS protocols, and regular security audits. Your data is protected and we comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other international standards.

Can I use multiple team members on one account?

Yes! With PageoNix Professional and Enterprise plans, you can add team members with different permission levels. Manage who can access, edit, and publish your content.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Starter plan features. No credit card required to start. Experience PageoNix risk-free!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers. Subscriptions renew automatically each month or year.
